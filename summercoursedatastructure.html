Summer Course on Data Structures

Enhance your programming skills with our comprehensive Data Structures course!

Course Overview

Our Summer Course on Data Structures is perfect for those looking to solidify their understanding of data organization and manipulation. This course is designed for both beginners and intermediate learners.

Course Schedule

Duration: 6 weeks

Start Date: June 1, 2024

End Date: July 13, 2024

Classes: Mondays and Wednesdays, 5 PM - 8 PM

Syllabus Highlights:

  • Introduction to Data Structures
  • Arrays and Linked Lists
  • Stacks and Queues
  • Trees and Binary Trees
  • Graphs and Algorithms
  • Hashing Techniques
  • Sorting and Searching Algorithms
  • Final project to implement data structures in a practical application

By the end of this course, you will have the skills to design and implement complex data structures effectively!
